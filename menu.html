    • The Courtship Dance of the endangered Kinabalu Birdwing Butterfly. The males are much smaller, with black front wings. The females are much larger with white front wings. The female flies around in a circle. The male flies above her, very close but not touching. He wafts down perfume scent scales to capture her interest. Compiled and Produced by Stephen Sutton.
